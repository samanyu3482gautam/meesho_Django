Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to become a supplier? +

To become a supplier, you need to have a registered business (sole proprietorship, partnership, or private limited company), GSTIN number, and a product catalog with at least 10 unique products.

Is there any registration fee? +

No, registering as a supplier on Meesho is completely free. There are no hidden charges or fees.

How do I get paid for my sales? +

Payments are processed weekly and transferred directly to your registered bank account. You'll receive payment within 7 days of order delivery.

Who handles shipping and delivery? +

As a supplier, you're responsible for shipping products to customers. However, we provide shipping labels and partner with logistics providers to make the process easier.

Can I sell internationally through Meesho? +

Currently, Meesho only supports domestic sales within India. We may expand to international markets in the future.
